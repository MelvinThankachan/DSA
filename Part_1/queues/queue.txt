Queue

1. Printers - To manage workflow
2. Operating Systems - To manage processes
3. Web servers - To manage requests
4. Customer service systems - To manage the enquiries

Queue can be used anywhere to perform actions in the order the inputs received.

1. FIFO(First In First Out)
2. Addition perform in one end and deletion performs in the other end

Operations:
1. enqueue - O(1)
2. dequeue - O(1)
3. peek - O(1)
4. is_empty - O(1)
4. is_full - O(1)
